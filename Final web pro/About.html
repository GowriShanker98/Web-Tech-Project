Cake Delight
For New york Times, The 'Best Baker' of Cake Delight, mixes eggs, sugar, butter and flour means a lot more than "making a cake". As a fourth generation baker, it's a constant source of pleasure, pride and creativity. Creating amazing cakes connects Cake Delight with the people. Cake Delight had always been well known for its incredible wedding cakes and over the years, Cake Delight creations were frequently featured in bridal magazines. As a result of this exposure, Cake Delight has participated in Food Network Challenge in 2004. Recognizing a growing interest in making this bakery an Official one, Cake Delight saw an oppurtunity to push the bakkery into new official Cake Delight and it jumped at the chance. A Naturally outgoing "Bakery" now build an official Cafe. It completed in four Food Network Challenge cake competitions. After losing its first three battles, Cake Delight took top prize on "Battle of the Brides". Along with a cash prize of $1000 has checque which proudly hangs on the wall of its cafe. Cake Delight is a top growing cafe in the World.
